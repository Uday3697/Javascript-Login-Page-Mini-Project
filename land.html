Welcome To VBU

Vinoba Bhave University was established in the year 1992 with its headquarters at Hazaribag, the Divisional headquarter of North Chhotanagpur division of the state of Jharkhand.

Vinoba Bhave University is imparting post-graduate teaching and research in Physical Science, Life Science, Earth Science, Social Science, Humanities, Commerce, Technology and Medical Science.
